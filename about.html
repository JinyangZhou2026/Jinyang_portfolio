About / Motivation

Designing the bridge between engineering and understanding.

Personal Statement

I am interested in what happens when complex products need to become clear to people.

My background in industrial design taught me to understand products through form, function, interaction, and manufacturing constraints. Working in an engineering-led environment expanded that perspective: a product also needs to be explained accurately to the teams, customers, and markets around it.

I use product visualization and technical communication to connect engineering information with customer understanding. My work moves between CAD, rendering, animation, information hierarchy, and final communication materials while keeping the product’s technical logic visible.

I am looking to contribute within industrial and product-focused organizations where design, engineering, marketing, and international communication work together to move complex products forward.
