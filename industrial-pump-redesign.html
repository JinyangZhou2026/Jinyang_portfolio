Industrial Pump Design Optimization

A series of professional industrial design projects focused on optimizing the appearance, consistency, and visual identity of industrial pump product families.

Working in close collaboration with engineering teams, I refined external components and product architecture while respecting mechanical requirements, manufacturing constraints, assembly logic, and existing production processes. The work aimed to improve product coherence across different models, strengthen brand recognition, and enhance the overall quality of customer-facing products without compromising engineering performance or production feasibility.
