Brief

Research identifies a domestic need for plant care that is easier to access, monitor, and integrate into everyday furniture.

The concept combines shelving, storage, and plant display into a compact vertical product for the bedroom.

Function

Modular plant zones and storage elements organize care, access, and daily use around a single furniture object.

Plant Benefits

The selected plants bring practical benefits into the home, from fragrance and air quality to compact edible growth.

Assembly & Components

The product breaks down into frame, shelf, planting, and drawer components for clearer assembly and maintenance.

In-Context Renderings

Interior renderings evaluate scale, placement, and product behavior in a realistic domestic context.

Study Models

Physical models test scale, proportion, and shelf rhythm before the final visualization direction.
